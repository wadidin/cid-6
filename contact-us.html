EVENT INFORMATION

Conferences dedicated to building remarkable events.

This year, Indonesian Diaspora Network Global (IDN-Global) is thrilled to present the the 6th Congress of Indonesian Diaspora (CID-6) in August 2021. CID is a biannual event to connect Indonesians living abroad with their tanah air or homeland and serve as a platform for them to contribute to national development. With our current theme “Post Covid-19: A Stronger Indonesia with Diaspora”, we believe Indonesia could emerge even stronger after this challenging pandemic with the active contribution and participation of different stakeholders including the Indonesian diaspora.
